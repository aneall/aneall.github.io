About Me

My name is Ashley Neall, and I'm a Junior at UNC-Chapel Hill studying Computer Science, Neuroscience, and Applied Engineering. I'm passionate about computer graphics, computational displays (imaging, optics, haptics, BCIs), and human perception – all of which are crucial to improve XR systems. You'll find me researching while leading Carolina AR/VR and Intercollegiate XR. In my free time, I still develop, but I also design and teach :)


Fun fact: my passion for XR began in high school, where I created Apex Immersive – the first XR student organization in North Carolina's largest public school system. My leadership endeavors led me to revitalize Carolina AR/VR as a freshman in college and dive into research.
