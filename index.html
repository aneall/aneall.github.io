Hello there, I'm Ashley.

My name is Ashley Neall, and I'm a Senior at UNC-Chapel Hill studying Computer Science, Neuroscience, and Applied Engineering. My research co-designs computational displays, sensing, and rendering paradigms for human sensation and perception. I am broadly interested in building multisensory extended reality (XR) systems that empower people through efficiency and interactivity.


You'll find me researching while leading Carolina AR/VR and Intercollegiate XR. In my free time, I develop, design, and teach :)


My passion for XR began in high school, where I created Apex Immersive – the first XR student organization in North Carolina's largest public school system. My leadership endeavors led me to revitalize Carolina AR/VR as a freshman in college and dive into research.
